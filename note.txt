Outliers:

The total dataset will be divided into 4 quarters LIKE -- DATA ORIGIN -- Q1 -- MEDIAN -- Q3 -- DATA END

Q1 = 25TH PERCENTILE 
Q2 = 50TH PERCENTILE OR MEDIAN 
Q3 = 75TH PERCENTILE 

Inter Quartile Range IQR = (Q3 - Q1)

OUTLIER RULE :
 
 upper bound --> Q3 + 1.5 * IQR
 inner bound --> Q1 - 1.5 * IQR 
Anything outside these limits would be considered as an outlier 


MEAN Is Usually the average. MOSTLY TAKEN FOR MOST OF THE DATABASE 

IF MEAN > MEDIAN RIGHT SKEWED --> POSITIVE SKEWED 

IF MEAN < MEDIAN LEFT SKEWED --> NEGATIVE SKEWED 

TYPES: ARITHEMATIC, GEOMETRIC , HARMONIC 

ARITHEMATIC --> sum(all data points) / number of data points  ==> MOST DATASETS 
Geometric --> nth root over product of all data points  ==> CAGR , GROWTH RATES 
Harmonic --> (n * product of all the data points) / sum of all data points  ==> SPEED , RATES 

usually AM >= GM >= HM

VARIANCE. POPULATION == (sum of (all data points - mean)squared ) divided by the count of data points 

variance.sample usually preferred == (sum(all data points - mean))squared divided by number of data points -1 

standard deviation =  sqrt(variance
